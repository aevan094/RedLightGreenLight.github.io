Red Light, Light Green

Rules

Move when the light is green, don't move when the light is red.
If you move when the light is red, you lose immediately.
To win, make it to the end of the platform.
You have 3 lives to spend hitting obstacles. Don't waste them.
The rate that obstacles spawn increases the longer you take to get to end.

Player Controls

A or Arrow Left: Move Left
D or Arrow Right: Move Right
W or Arrow Up: Move Forward

Score: 0
Lives: 3
YOU LOSE! Game will reset in 3
YOU WIN! Game will reset in 3
